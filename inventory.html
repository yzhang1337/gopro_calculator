GoPro Setup Calculator

Currently there is a deal on the Hero Black 11 Mini at GoPro.com. I have no experience with this particular camera as we used/using GoPro 8/9 just because that is what was 'out' then, but now we are up to GoPro 11. The mini seems like a good deal though, the only major con is that it does NOT have a swappable battery, which might be inconvenient but we could also just run a charging cable to it instead of swapping batteries. The benefit of using any GoPro model 10 or greater is that the maximum microSD card allowed is 4x the GoPro 8/9 (256 GB) at 1TB. Obviously there may be some cost/benefit to be done here depending on which setup scales better for any given situation so feel free to play around with the calculator to get a sense of what might be cheapest now (July 2023) versus best long-term solution.

* Note: For reference... 256GB records ~6.5 hours of video at 4k@60FPS OR ~10.5 hours of video at 1080p@60FPS and 1TB records ~26 hours of video at 4k@60FPS OR ~42 hours of video at 1080p@60FPS. Obviously, microSD cards of any size below 1TB is also compatible with the Hero 10/11.
